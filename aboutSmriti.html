About Smriti

Hi,
I am Smriti Mool from Nepal, currently staying at 325 Spruce Street, Waterloo, Canada. I've completed my Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering and have 4.5 years of professional experience as a full-stack developer.

As a developer, programmer, or software engineer, I enjoy using various programming languages to create computer software. I have a learning and never-giving-up attitude and prefer being updated with recent technologies. I like being in in-charge of understanding the requirements and building the appropriate solutions. Regarding my prior work experience, I was involved in solving complicated business problems using both back-end technologies such as JAVA and databases and front-end technologies such as Angular and ReactJS. With such hands-on experience, I have become a critical thinker, a strong problem-solver, and the ability to use more than one programming language along with designing, testing, and developing the software considering the client's requirements.

Besides, I also have good written and communication skills and strategies, which makes me convenient to communicate with supervisors and clients. It feels great to say that the web browser, web pages, and so many innovative products to market would have been impossible without the dedication of developers, and I enjoy every bit and piece of being a part of this participation. I can go out of the box to suggest solutions and functionalities that would otherwise be very time-consuming, missing requirements such as exception handling and user experience, prioritizing customer security and safety, and recommend creative opportunities to take advantage of all modern technologies. In addition, I have worked as a Certified Scrum Master and as a developer. This dual responsibility has prepared me to work under pressure, prioritize tasks, remove any impediments throughout the process, and ensure the product backlog is in the right direction. I utterly value the concept of teamwork and coaching the team members in self-management and cross-functionalities. As a developer representing the delivery team, I have the traits of becoming a leader of my own or the team.
